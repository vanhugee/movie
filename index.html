How will we measure movie performance?

To measure movie performance, we focus on a few key variables: revenue (global box office earnings), budget (the cost of production), and popularity (public reception). Additionally, we will categorize movies by genre and release year, t o explore trends within specific categories and over time. We will also examine production countries to gain insights into regional contributions to the film industry. By leveraging these metrics, we aim to provide both neutral and persuasive visualizations that reveal insights into the factors driving success in cinema.
